Who I Am

I am a brewer turned web developer who can not only tell you why your code isn’t working but can also tell you which malts are likely in that pint you’re drinking. Since 1998, I have been familiarizing myself with as many programming languages and technologies as I can find, including HTML, CSS, C++, Ruby, and most recently full-stack web development. With a strong eye for detail and a “job’s not done until it’s done right” attitude, I now look forward to making the world a better place one program, and pint, at a time.

For the Peopleclose

An app designed to increase civic participation by providing a massive amount of government information in one place. This app uses the Google Civic Information API, ProPublica API, and the user's provided address to:

  • List a user's representatives through all levels of government with links to their office webpage
  • Create a custom page for congressional representatives that lists their contact information, voting tendencies, and recent voting activity
  • Show upcoming local and national elections with a map showing their designated polling location
  • Provide a short list of current congressional voting activity
